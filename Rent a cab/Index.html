Transitioning Smoothly from Renting to Owning

Simplify your move from renting to owning with our easy guide. Choose, plan, share, and collect your ideal car hassle-free

1) Select Your Ideal Ride

Choose from our carefully curated range of brand new cars, each tailored to meet your private hire needs. Find the perfect vehicle that aligns with your style and preferences.

2) Pick Your Ownership Plan

Option for a 4 or 5-year plan that suits your goals. Enjoy the flexibility of a timeline that works for you, making ownership an achievable reality.

3) Share Your Details

Provide us with your essential details, and our streamlined process will get your ownership journey started. We make it easy for you to transition from renting to owning

4) Car on Order, Prep Underway

After selecting your car and providing your details, we take over. Your chosen vehicle is ordered and meticulously prepared by our team, handling all logistics while you anticipate owning your new ride.

5) Collect and Begin Your Journey

The moment arrives – it's time to collect your newly prepared car. Start your ownership journey with confidence, knowing everything's taken care of. Simply pay your weekly rate and relish calling the car yours.

FAQ

Answers to Your Questions

What’s the difference with you and other taxi rental companies ?

We offer Rent to Buy , So what we are offering is that you get a brand new car of your choice and pay rent every week for 5 yrs and the car is yours , It allows the cab driver to own the car which has been well looked after . With us you will get 2 weeks rent free every year , You will have a fully comprehensive insurance direct under the driver name , Unlimited miles , Service every 12,000 miles and 4 new tyres every year

Please explain the Private hire Insurance that you provide ?

We provide a fully comprehensive Insurance that covers private hire and social domestic usage , Its directly under the drivers name which is a service not provided by other cab rental companies , The reason for us to provide this service helps the driver build his no claim bonus each year . So after 5 yrs your insurance no claim will reward you when you eventually own the car after 5 yrs

Why have you got a limited car selection ?

We are working towards getting more cars added soon

What’s the eligibility criteria ?

Full UK licence , Council taxi badge PH or Hackney Carraige , No more than 6 points on licence , Over 21 yrs old , Minimum 1 yr old taxi badge

What does the car come with ?

It will be a brand new car With Leather seats , Trackers and cameras are standard , Rubber matts and a spare tyre Kit . Roadside recovery is also included for the entire duration of the contract

What happens if I change my mind after a while ?

Its simple you return the car . If the rent is not paid for 2 consecutive weeks , we will recover the car . If you change your mind at any point you can simply return the car and whatever monies have been paid will be kept as the rent for that duration . In order to own the car you have to complete a full 5 yr term

Is there a contract to sign ?

Yes , a full rent to buy contract stating all the terms and conditions

What areas are you offering this service ?

We offer this service to all parts of England
